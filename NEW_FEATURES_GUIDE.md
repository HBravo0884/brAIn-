## Feature 1: Auto-Fill Your Information

### What It Does
Automatically fills in your common information (name, department, etc.) in all forms, saving you time on every payment request, travel form, and report.

### How to Set Up

1. **Click "Settings"** in the sidebar (bottom of navigation)

2. **Fill in your profile**:
   - **Full Name** (e.g., Dr. John Smith)
   - **Title/Position** (e.g., Program Manager)
   - **Department** (e.g., College of Medicine)
   - **Institution** (e.g., Howard University College of Medicine)
   - **Email** (your work email)
   - **Phone** (your contact number)
   - **Default Grant Aim** (e.g., Aim 5 - Re-Entry) - Optional but useful!
   - **PI Name** (Principal Investigator)
   - **PI Email** (PI's email for approvals)

3. **Click "Save Profile"**

4. **Done!** Your information now auto-fills in:
   - Payment Request Forms
   - Travel Authorization Forms
   - Gift Card Distribution Logs
   - KPI Progress Reports
   - Budget Justifications

### How It Works

**Before:**
- Every time you create a payment request, you manually type:
  - Your name
  - Your department
  - Select grant aim
  - etc.

**After:**
- Open payment request form
- Your name, department, and default aim are already filled in!
- Just enter the specific details (vendor, amount, purpose)
- Save 2-3 minutes per form!

### Example

**Creating a Payment Request:**
1. Go to Payment Requests ‚Üí New Payment Request
2. **Auto-filled for you:**
   - Requestor: Dr. John Smith ‚úì
   - Department: College of Medicine ‚úì
   - Grant Aim: Aim 5 - Re-Entry ‚úì
3. **You only fill in:**
   - Expense Type: Gift Cards
   - Vendor: Visa
   - Amount: $500
   - Purpose: Participant incentives

### Privacy
- All data stored locally in your browser
- Never sent to external servers
- You can clear it anytime with "Reset" button

## Feature 2: Use Documents as Templates

### What It Does
Mark any uploaded document (like your existing PRF forms) as a template, then create copies to reuse without altering the original.

### Perfect For
- Your existing PRF forms
- Standard travel request forms
- Any document you use repeatedly
- Forms with pre-filled information
- Howard University templates

### How to Use

#### Step 1: Upload Your Document
1. Go to **Documents** page
2. Click **"Upload Documents"**
3. Upload your PRF form or other template document
4. Select category and upload

#### Step 2: Mark as Template
1. Find your uploaded document
2. Click the **Star icon** (‚≠ê) button
3. Document is now marked as a template!
   - Yellow star appears on the document
   - "Template" badge shows

#### Step 3: Use Your Template
1. Click **"My Document Templates"** tab to see all your templates
2. Find the template you want to use
3. Click **"Use Template"** button
4. A copy is created (named "Document Name (Copy)")
5. Download or view the copy
6. Fill it out as needed
7. **Original template stays unchanged!**

### Visual Indicators

**Regular Document:**
- Blue file icon
- No star

**Document Template:**
- Yellow star icon (‚≠ê)
- "Template" badge
- Yellow border
- "Use Template" button

### Example Workflow

**Your "Gift Cards PRF 02-17-26.pdf":**

1. **Upload** the PDF to Documents page
2. **Mark as Template** by clicking star icon
3. **Next time you need a gift card PRF:**
   - Go to "My Document Templates" tab
   - Click "Use Template" on your PRF
   - A copy is created
   - Fill out the copy for new request
   - Original PRF template is unchanged!

### Benefits

**No Alterations:**
- Original document never changes
- Create unlimited copies
- Each copy is independent
- Perfect for forms with formatting

**Quick Reuse:**
- No need to re-upload
- No need to recreate
- One click to create a copy
- Consistent formatting

**Organization:**
- Separate templates from regular documents
- Easy to find your reusable forms
- Star icon for quick identification

### Managing Templates

**To Remove Template Status:**
- Click the star icon again
- Document becomes regular document
- Still visible in "All Documents"

**To Delete a Template:**
- Click trash icon
- Confirms deletion
- All copies remain (they're independent)

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### Auto-Fill Not Working?
1. Go to Settings page
2. Verify profile information is saved
3. Look for green "Profile saved!" message
4. Try creating new payment request

### Template Not Appearing?
1. Check "My Document Templates" tab
2. Verify star icon is filled (yellow)
3. If not, click star icon to mark as template

### Can't Edit Template?
- **Don't edit the template directly!**
- Click "Use Template" to create a copy
- Edit the copy instead
